# Delicious Chocolate Cake Recipe

## Ingredients:
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, instant coffee, chocolate chips, vanilla extract,
and ground cinnamon.
3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and add them to the mixture. Stir until well combined.
4. Line the bottom of the baking pan with parchment paper for easy cake removal.
5. Gradually add the milk and vegetable oil to the mixture, continuing to stir.
6. Pour the batter into a greased and floured 9x13-inch baking pan.
